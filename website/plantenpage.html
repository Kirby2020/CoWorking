Planten

Sunflowers zijn nodig voor extra zon te produceren waardoor je meer planten gaat kunnen plaatsen. Dus ze zijn niet enkel mooi maar ook vrij handig, Plant er zo veel als je kan.

health: 100hp
tijd nodig om zon te produceren:
5s

Peashooters zijn de belangrijkste planten in defensie. Ze komen namelijk het vaakst voor op de eerste lijn. Ze schieten erwten naar zombies die dan op hun beurt 10 hp afnemen van de zombie zijn health.

health: 100hp
attackdamage: 10hp
cooldown: 10s

Repeaters zijn een variant van de peashooters maar doe dat maal 2. Hij doet namelijk alles 2 keer zo veel. 2 erwten schieten, 2 keer hetzelfde zeggen, ...

health: 100hp
attackdamage: 20hp
cooldown: 10s

Snow Peas zijn een variant van de peashooters maar maak dat 50 graden kouder. Wanneer hij een zombie raakt beweegt deze zombie trager waardoor je meer tijd hebt om terug te vechten.

health: 100hp
attackdamage: 10hp
cooldown: 10s

Wall-nuts have hard shells which you can use to protect your other plants. Having retired from his days as a champion bowler in the Pro Leagues, Wall-nut is looking forward to a real challenge on the front lines of home defense. DEFENSE! DEFENSE!

Tall-nuts are heavy-duty wall plants that block low flying zombies. Tall-nut is opening a chain of stores for Tall and Taller gentlemen. "I'll shell out the savings to you!"

Potato Mines take time to arm before exploding on the unlucky zombie who steps on them. He is frequently given rodent-like features and characteristics, with him even performing rodent-like squeaking in the shooter games.

Cherry Bombs can blow up all zombies in an area. They have a short fuse so plant them near zombies. The Cherry Bomb Brothers attempted to start their own band. “We tried to figure out what the sound would be, but we kept explodonating speakers, stages, fans, and of course, zombies. Look for our LP next year.”

Chompers can devour a zombie whole, but they are vulnerable while chewing. Everyone appreciates what Chomper does. His appetite has saved them on numerous occasions. But truth be told, Chomper's friends think that eating zombies is gross. And his breath! No one has the heart to tell him, and he never gets the hint when you offer him a mint.

Squashes will smash the first zombie that gets close. Squash is actually his stage name. His birth name was Tracy.

Jalapenos destroy an entire lane of zombies. This jalapeno is one spicy character by day and The Macramé Vigilante by night. You might be wondering what his secret weapon is… it's knots.

Pumpkin is a defensive plant who can protect other plants inside of him. Pumpkin is confirmed in Plants vs. Zombies to be related to Renfield, a Peggle Master who appears in the Peggle series, also by Popcap. This is the only direct link to other games by Popcap, as other links are either simple cameos or similar names.

Torchwoods turn peas that pass through them into fireballs that deal twice as much damage. Torchwood and Snapdragon have become fast friends. They like to hang out at either the Tiki Lounge or the Renaissance Faire.
